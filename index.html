Here, he dons the mantle of a mechanical engineering student, immersing himself in the intricacies of design, production, and industrial engineering. MNNIT, renowned for its excellence, provides the perfect canvas for Harsh to explore the world of gears, machines, and innovation. Whether defending or raiding, Harsh’s commitment to the game is unwavering, echoing the spirit of Uttarakhand’s rugged terrain. But Harsh’s talents extend beyond the physical realm. He wields the keyboard with finesse, crafting elegant lines of code. As a proficient coder, he navigates the digital landscape.
